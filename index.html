Logitech Headset Comfort Study

Objective

The study aims to comprehend how female gamers differ from male gamers, identifying their unique pain points and proposing solutions. By focusing on ear pad improvement, this iterative research seeks to address specific discomforts and enhance the overall gaming experience for female gamers.

Methodology

I utilized survey, diary studies, and user interviews to gather qualitative and quantitative data from 5 regular headset users in order to identify pain points and preferences.

Confidentiality Notice

This study is protected under a non-disclosure agreement (NDA). Further details can be shared during an interview upon request.
